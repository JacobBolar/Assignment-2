Grilled Cheese w/ Bacon & Tomato Recipe:

List of Ingredients:

  • Two pieces of bread
  • Two slices of Cheese
  • 3 Pieces of Bacon
  • One slice of Tomato

First, lightly toast your two pieces of bread. Next, place them on a pan on low heat with the cheese on top. You can also start to cook the bacon at this time. Once the bread is nice and the cheese is melted, take off the two pieces of bread. Place a slice of tomato and bacon on one of the pieces of bread. Combine the two pieces of bread and you are done!
